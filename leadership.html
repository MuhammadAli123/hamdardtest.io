Founder

We have each come into this world for a specific reason – to live, love and serve Allah through service of His creatures.This is what the institution of Hamdard reflects – dignity, knowledge, faith, sincerity, honesty and the spirit of service.

Hamdard (signifying one who has ‘sympathy’) has chosen to serve through activities for the promotion of good health and education – the twin pillars of progress.

“Love Pakistan, Build Pakistan” should be our motto.


How can we express it better than through faith and service, which are the essence of humanity.

President

My father, Shaheed-e-Pakistan Hakim Mohammed Said, was a remarkable humanitarian, an eminent social scientist, a renowned physician and revivalist of Traditional Medicine, who had lent a new dimension to Alternative Medicine having getting it recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO).Hamdard Laboratories (Waqf), Hamdard Foundation Pakistan, Hamdard Clinics and Madinat al-Hikmah – a city of science, culture and education, are a testimony of his love for humanity,devotion to the promotion of education and learning and dedicated service to the people of Pakistan.

His struggle and mission in pursuit of the well-being of the poor and betterment for Pakistan will continue to inspire us. Love Pakistan, Build Pakistan, was the watchword that he gave to the people of Pakistan.

I pledge myself to continue his mission under all circumstances.

President : Sadia Rashid

Vice President

Hamdard, a tibbi pharmaceutical industry was established in Pakistan in 1948 by Shaheed Hakim Mohammed Said. In 1953 Shaheed Hakim Mohammed Said converted Hamdard into an Islamic Trust “Hamdard Laboratories (Waqf)” devoting its income in the name of Al-mighty Allah to fulfill his dream of service to humanity.

To administer and control the welfare works of Hamdard Laboratories (Waqf) separately and independently, Hamdard Foundation Pakistan was established in 1964. It has now grown into a multidimensional institution which is whole heartedly devoted to the promotion of health, education and social welfare.

Read More

The funds generated by Hamdard Laboratories (Waqf) are diverted to Hamdard Foundation Pakistan for health, philanthropic and educational objectives like:


  • Free consultation and medicine, through a network of clinics and mobile dispensaries throughout major cities of Pakistan
  • Scholarships and freeships for students who merit them, either through brilliance or need.
  • Establishing Madinat al-Hikmah – the city of science, education and culture – at Deh Bund Murad, on the outskirts of Karachi. This houses the Hamdard Public School, Hamdard Village School (free), Hamdard University with its many faculties and Institutes, and Bait al-Hikmah, one of Asia’s largest libraries.
  • Stipends for various needy persons, such as poor widows, and grants to various national institutions.
  • Publications of journals and books on subsidized rates.
  • Cultural activities from the platform of Shura Hamdard and Naunehal Assembly

Hamdard Foundation Pakistan, being a charitable organization, has been performing welfare, charitable and educational activities since its establishment in 1964. We hope that through our sincere efforts and the help/guidance of Allah, we would achieve the objectives & dreams of Shaheed Hakim Mohammed Said.

President : Dr Navaid-ul-Zafar

Director General

Everybody in Pakistan is doubtless familiar with the name of Hamdard, which was established in Pakistan in 1948 by Shaheed Hakim Mohammed Said. What everybody may possibly not be aware of is that Shaheed Hakim Mohammed Said transformed Hamdard Laboratories, in 1953, into an Islamic Waqf, “Hamdard Laboratories (Waqf)”, so that its entire profits go towards funding charitable, educational and nation-building activities.
