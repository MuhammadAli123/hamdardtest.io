PUBLICATION

Hamdard Foundation Pakistan is engaged for more than half century in the publication of books and magazines, both Urdu and English in the fields of Islamic Studies, Education, Literature, History, Library, Information Science, Medicine, History of Medicine, Philosophy, Science, Sociology, Children’s and Youth Literature. It has so far published nearly 700 titles in Urdu and English with the object providing works which are authoritative and of outstanding quality to the reading clientele both within the country and abroad.

One of the distinctive features of the Hamdard’s Publications is their prices which are kept below the cost and the loss is covered by a subsidy of Hamdard Foundation Pakistan, thereby assuring that their Publications will secure the widest possible distribution.

Hamdard Naunehal


Shaheed Hakim Mohammad Said always stressed on character building along with regular education. He started Monthly Hamdard Naunehal to achieve this goal. Alhamdulillah, Naunehal, with guidance of its Editor In Chief, Mrs. Sadia Rashid is still going strong. Mr. Masood Ahmed Barakati, who served Naunehal for over 50 years and made it No 1 selling children monthly is now retired due to ill health.

Read More

Circulation numbers stayed steady during last couple of years Despite our best efforts to increase. Reasons are digitalization and children’s ever increasing interest in online contents than printed materials. We have started providing free PDF format of Hamdard Naunehal on HFP’s website for children all over the world to read. However we only provide a month old Naunehal online in order to safe guard the sale of the printed version.
